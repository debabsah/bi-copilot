- **What this is for:** report retention health to the board each quarter, and
  inform one annual decision, whether to fund a dedicated onboarding / CS team to
  reduce early-life churn.
- **Who consumes it:** the board (quarterly) and the exec team (annual budget).
- **The ask vs the real decision:** the ask arrived as "a churn dashboard (logo
  churn, MRR churn, a 12-month cohort curve, by plan tier)." The real decision is
  *invest in fixing early churn vs invest in new-logo growth*, which turns on net
  revenue retention and early-life churn, not logo churn. See `requirements-brief.md`.
